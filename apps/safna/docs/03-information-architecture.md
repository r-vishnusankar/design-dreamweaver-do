# Information Architecture

```
Safna Wedding Invitation
├── Entry Layer
│   ├── Sound Gate ("Begin the Experience")
│   └── Opening Animation
├── Chapter 01 — Welcome (Hero)
├── Chapter 02 — A Letter from Safna
├── Chapter 03 — Her Journey (Timeline)
├── Chapter 04 — Their Story (+ Groom link)
├── Chapter 05 — Reception Details
├── Chapter 06 — Venue
├── Chapter 07 — Family
├── Chapter 08 — Gallery
├── Chapter 09 — Countdown
├── Chapter 10 — Blessings (API)
├── Chapter 11 — Thank You (+ Seal Easter Egg)
└── Global Layers
    ├── Jasmine petal ambient
    ├── Cursor petals (desktop)
    ├── Night mode (after sunset)
    ├── Mute toggle
    └── Lenis smooth scroll
```

## Content Hierarchy

| Priority | Content | Rationale |
|----------|---------|-----------|
| P0 | Date, time, venue | Guest must find logistics |
| P0 | Bride identity & families | Cultural expectation |
| P1 | Personal letter | Differentiator |
| P1 | Countdown | Engagement |
| P2 | Gallery, blessings | Delight & interaction |
| P3 | Easter eggs | Memorable moments |
